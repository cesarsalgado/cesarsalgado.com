I felt in love on computers when I was 14. Since, I never stopped to learn new things and I hope I'll not stop anytime soon!

However, I'm not just a geek, I love culture and especially History too. And I love to read books.

How Do I Work

When I have to work on a project, I start it from scratch because each client has specific needs and require a website tailored to his purposes. That's why I start from scratch to go straight to the essential.

I hate having to work lonely without any contacts with my client. I think the client must take part to the project directly. When I've finished a feature, I plan a meeting with my client to discuss what I've done and to see if everything is how the client want it to be.

Most of the time, I work alone but I'm always open to propositions to work with a team. I love to use GitHub and Basecamp to work with other developers.
