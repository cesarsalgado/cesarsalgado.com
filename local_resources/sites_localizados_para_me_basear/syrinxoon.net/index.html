Method

Design and Development Workflow

  • Your Idea

    Your project starts by one or more face-to-face discussions (if possible) during which we talk about the idea behind it. Theses discussions allow me to understand exactly your needs, your business and put myself in the skin of a user of your website so I can ask myself the good questions later in the project.

  • Planning

    Once your project discussed, we plan a few key milestones. Theses milestones give us an estimate of the time needed to realize your project. Then, we plan to see each other once per week or every few weeks so we can iterate on your project in an agile way.

  • Create

    I will create your project with the technologies we defined together. Whatever they are, your project will be tests driven and versioned so you can be sure you'll get something clean at the end. Moreover, you will be given an access to my Basecamp account to keep track of my progress and discuss everything you need.

  • Deliver

    When we will reach the end of the project, I will help you to deploy your application wherever you want whether it is on a dedicated server or AWS EC2. My applications come with a warranty of one year including maintenance and bug fixes.
